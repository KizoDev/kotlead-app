RESULT CHECKING

19th December 2018

Good day sir/ma! Kindly follow the steps below in checking your child's result
1. log on to www.shalomacademy.net
2. click on check result
3. input your child registration number in the first dialog box
4. scratch off the silver panel of the card gently
5. enter the PIN number to the second dialog box and serial number to the third dialog box
6. click on submit and then click on result.
please ensure you input your child registration number correctly.
